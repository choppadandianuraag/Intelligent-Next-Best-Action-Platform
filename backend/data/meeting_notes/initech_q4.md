# Meeting Notes — Initech Q4 Re-onboarding (Resolved Case)
**Date:** 2025-11-20
**Participants:** Casey Morgan (CSM), Phil Davison (COO, Initech), New team leads x3
**Duration:** 2 hours — Re-onboarding Session
**Account:** Initech | ARR: $78,000
**Status:** RESOLVED — Renewed and Expanded

---

**Context:** Initech experienced a major team restructuring in Q3 2025. Three department heads left and new leads inherited the platform with no training. DAU dropped from 38 to 11. Health score fell from 76 to 44. Churn risk flagged as high.

---

**Phil:** Casey, I'll be straight — my new team leads have inherited this platform and none of them know how to use it. I'm getting questions I can't answer. We're paying for something we're barely using.

**Casey:** Phil, thank you for flagging this directly. This is exactly the situation where we need to step in. I've set up today's session to be a hands-on re-onboarding — not slides, but actually working in the platform together.

**New Lead 1 (Marcus Webb, Ops):** I've logged in maybe three times. I don't even know where to start.

**Casey:** That's completely fine. Let's start with the three workflows that will give you value in the first week. I'm sharing my screen — Marcus, I want you to follow along on your own instance.

*(Casey runs through: dashboard setup → alert configuration → first automation workflow)*

**Marcus:** Oh. That's actually really intuitive once you see it.

**New Lead 2 (Diana Park, Analytics):** The reporting module — can I build a report right now?

**Casey:** Yes. Let's do it together. Tell me what data you need to see.

*(Diana and Casey build a custom report live)*

**Phil:** Casey, this is what I was hoping for. Is there a way to have recordings of this session for team members who couldn't join?

**Casey:** I'm recording this session and I'll send you the edited version along with written guides for everything we covered.

**New Lead 3 (James Okonkwo, Customer Success):** The automation — can it pull from our HubSpot?

**Casey:** Yes, and I'll walk you through the HubSpot connector right now.

**Phil:** Casey, if my team gets to where they were by year-end, we'll renew. And if we hit the targets we talked about, I'd be open to expanding seats.

---

**Outcome:** Team re-onboarded successfully. DAU recovered from 11 to 43 within 8 weeks. Health score recovered to 71. Renewed on schedule with 8 additional seats ($12K expansion). Re-onboarding session + recordings was the critical intervention.

**CSM Notes:** When champions/power users leave en masse, don't send documentation — run live hands-on sessions immediately. Record everything. Follow up with written summaries. New users need to experience quick wins in the first session.
