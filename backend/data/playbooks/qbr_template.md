# QBR Template

## Overview
The Quarterly Business Review (QBR) is a structured, scheduled touchpoint with the customer's executive team. Unlike the EBR (which is reactive), the QBR is proactive and relationship-building. Run QBRs for all accounts with ARR >$50K.

## Scheduling
- Schedule 4–6 weeks in advance
- 60–90 minutes for standard QBR
- Required attendees: CSM, CSM Manager (or VP for strategic accounts), customer exec sponsor, customer day-to-day contact
- Send agenda 48 hours before

## QBR Agenda (90-minute format)

### 1. Welcome and Agenda Overview (5 min)
- Thank attendees for their time
- Confirm agenda and any last-minute priorities from the customer side

### 2. Quarter in Review — Business Outcomes (25 min)
Prepare slides covering:
- **Usage Metrics:** DAU trend, feature adoption heatmap, automation activity
- **ROI Summary:** Quantified value delivered (hours saved, issues caught, decisions accelerated)
- **Support Health:** Tickets opened vs. resolved, average resolution time
- **Milestones Hit:** From the success plan — which goals were achieved?
- **Milestones Missed:** Be transparent about what didn't go as planned

### 3. Customer Goals for Next Quarter (20 min)
Ask — don't assume. Questions to drive:
- What are your team's top 3 priorities for next quarter?
- Are there new initiatives or headcount changes coming?
- What does success look like for you 90 days from now?
- Are there capabilities you wish you had but don't?

### 4. Product Roadmap Alignment (15 min)
- Share relevant upcoming features (match to their stated goals)
- Beta program opportunities
- Get feedback on what they'd prioritize

### 5. Success Plan for Next Quarter (15 min)
- Update the mutual success plan based on the goals discussion
- Define 3–5 measurable milestones for Q+1
- Assign owners for each milestone
- Agree on check-in cadence

### 6. Any Other Business (10 min)
- Expansion discussion (if appropriate)
- Renewal preview (if within 6 months)
- Q&A and open floor

## QBR Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Usage report pulled and formatted for customer's audience
- [ ] ROI calculation completed with customer-specific numbers
- [ ] Success plan from last quarter reviewed
- [ ] 3 customer-specific roadmap items identified
- [ ] Renewal date confirmed and factored into agenda (if <6 months out)
- [ ] Executive sponsor confirmed to attend

## Post-QBR Actions (within 24 hours)
- Send meeting summary with all action items and owners
- Upload updated success plan to account record
- Log QBR outcome in CRM (positive/neutral/negative)
- If expansion discussed: start formal proposal process
