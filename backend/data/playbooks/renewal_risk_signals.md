# Renewal Risk Signals

## Overview
Identifying renewal risk early — ideally 90+ days before renewal date — is the difference between a save and a loss. This playbook catalogs the key signals CSMs should monitor, ranked by predictive power.

## High-Predictive-Power Signals (Act within 24–48 hours)

### Silence > 10 Days
An account where no key users have logged in for more than 10 consecutive days is a critical red flag. Historical data shows accounts with a >10-day silence gap are 4× more likely to churn than accounts with daily activity.
- **Action:** Same-day outreach to day-to-day contact. If no response within 48 hours, escalate to manager-level contact.

### Explicit Churn Threat
Any statement — verbal or written — that the customer is considering leaving.
- **Action:** P0 escalation. Same-day VP outreach.

### Competitor Mention
The customer mentions evaluating a competitor by name in any communication.
- **Action:** Do not dismiss or panic. Understand the specific capability gap driving the evaluation. Schedule an urgent product alignment call.

### Champion Departure
The internal champion has left the company or changed roles.
- **Action:** Follow Champion Change Playbook immediately.

### Support Ticket Backlog > 5 Open
Five or more unresolved tickets indicates systemic product issues or support failure.
- **Action:** Triage all tickets immediately. Escalate any ticket open >7 days to P1.

### NPS Score < 30
Any NPS below 30 indicates active detractors in the account.
- **Action:** Schedule 1:1 calls with detractors (don't use NPS survey responses alone — call them). Understand root cause.

## Medium-Predictive-Power Signals (Act within 3–5 business days)

### Health Score Declining 3+ Consecutive Weeks
A downward trend is more predictive than a single low score.
- **Action:** Schedule structured check-in, prepare custom health report.

### Feature Adoption Stalled at <3 Features
Low feature breadth means the customer hasn't found multiple value hooks.
- **Action:** Adoption expansion session — focus on 2 new features with clear ROI.

### No Automation Workflows Active After 60 Days
Automation is the stickiness feature — no workflows at day 60 indicates a shallow relationship.
- **Action:** Automation enablement session.

### Renewal Within 90 Days, No Renewal Conversation Started
If you haven't had an explicit renewal conversation and the date is within 90 days, start one today.
- **Action:** Schedule renewal discovery call. Identify economic buyer.

### Login Frequency Dropping Week-Over-Week
Even without a full silence gap, a consistent decline in login frequency is an early warning.
- **Action:** Proactive outreach to understand what's changed.

## Low-Predictive-Power Signals (Monitor, no immediate action)

- Single support ticket opened (normal)
- One missed check-in call (reschedule)
- User count slightly below licensed seats (normal for first 90 days)
- NPS 30–45 (monitor, plan a call within 2 weeks)

## Signal Combinations That Indicate High Churn Risk
The following signal combinations elevate risk dramatically:
- Silence > 10 days + renewal < 60 days = Critical
- Champion departure + support spike + low DAU = Critical
- Competitor evaluation + ROI question raised + NPS < 30 = Critical
- Any two "high-predictive-power" signals simultaneously = Escalate to P1 minimum
